I gave a presentation at the Amazon Health Services meeting on the future of healthcare AI.

The talk discussed why healthcare AI is reaching an inflection point, with accelerating progress across regulation, market activity, and research. At a high level, I covered emerging regulatory pathways for clinical AI, recent industry movement toward AI-enabled care models, and research trends in evaluation, benchmarking, and patient-facing agentic systems.
